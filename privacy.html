Privacy policy: Holtz Flooring

How we collect, use, and store information when you contact us about installation work.

Policy details

This policy applies to information submitted through the Holtz Flooring website and related email or telephone contact. Last updated April 2026.

Information we collect

When you submit a project enquiry, we collect the details you provide: name, business or company name, role, email address, phone number, project location, flooring type, and project description. If you contact us by phone or email directly, we retain the content required to respond to your enquiry and administer any subsequent contract.

Website analytics and cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 (Google LLC) to understand aggregate traffic and how visitors use our pages, and Microsoft Clarity (Microsoft Corporation) to improve usability. These services may set cookies and collect technical data such as device type, approximate location, and on-site behaviour in line with their respective privacy policies. You can control cookies through your browser settings. We use a Google Search Console verification tag only to confirm site ownership to Google; it is not used to profile individual visitors.

How we use information

We use contact and project information to respond to enquiries, prepare quotations, schedule installation work, and manage client relationships. We do not sell personal information. Analytics data is used only to operate and improve this website.

Storage and security

Enquiry data may be stored in business email, job management records, and secure backups appropriate to a small trade business. We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorised access. No online transmission is completely secure; if you are submitting highly sensitive documents, ask us for a secure transfer method.

Disclosure

We may disclose information where required by law, or to insurers and professional advisers in connection with legitimate business operations. We do not disclose your details to unrelated third parties for marketing.

Access and correction

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct inaccurate details, by contacting Holtz Flooring using the details on our contact page.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the enquiry form on contact.html or the phone and email listed in the site footer once those details are finalised for launch.
