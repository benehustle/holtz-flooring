Vinyl plank installation in Melbourne

LVP dominates offices, retail, and hospitality fit-outs. We install to commercial standards with attention to moisture resistance, acoustic underlay, and heavy-traffic detail at transitions.

Commercial application focus

Project managers specify LVP where durability, cleanability, and install speed matter. The category performs when expansion is planned at large footprints, wet areas are detailed correctly, and underlay matches acoustic targets set by tenancy or base build requirements.

We work with your drawings to set out feature strips, lift lobby interfaces, and kitchenette zones before adhesive or click systems go down. That reduces rework when services trades complete final connections.

What is involved

Moisture testing on slabs, preparation to flatness limits, and install method matched to the product — dry-back, loose lay, or click where approved for the environment. Acoustic underlay is installed to manufacturer stacking rules so performance claims remain valid.

We programme lifts and after-hours access where retail trading or office occupancy demands it, coordinating with your head contractor’s permits and inductions.

What to expect

Clear daily sign-off where required, protection of completed areas, and handover notes for facilities teams. If maintenance or pH-neutral cleaning is critical to warranty, we align with the supplier’s written guidance.

Benefits for your project

  • Moisture-aware installation suitable for commercial and wet-adjacent zones
  • Acoustic underlay installed to nominated performance stacks
  • Heavy-use detail at door lines, services, and public corridors

Frequently asked questions

Can LVP be installed in commercial kitchens or prep areas?

Only where the nominated product and adhesive system are rated for those conditions. We follow manufacturer wet-area detail and coving requirements. If the specification is ambiguous, we stop for written confirmation before proceeding.

How do you meet NCC-related acoustic expectations on a tenancy fit-out?

We install the underlay and floor build-up documented by your acoustic consultant or base build drawings. Field compliance is a design and testing matter — we execute the specified installation so your test sample reflects the as-built detail.

Do you work directly for fit-out contractors while products are supplied by a retailer?

Yes, with clear lines on delivery, batch numbers, and defect inspection before install. We remain installation-only and coordinate with both parties so programme and warranty records stay intact.
