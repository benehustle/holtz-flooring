Frequently asked questions

Can LVP be installed in commercial kitchens or prep areas?

Only where the nominated product and adhesive system are rated for those conditions. We follow manufacturer wet-area detail and installation requirements. If the specification is ambiguous, we stop for written confirmation before proceeding.

Do you work directly for fit-out contractors while products are supplied by a retailer?

Yes, with clear lines on delivery, batch numbers, and defect inspection before install. We remain installation-only and coordinate with both parties so programme and warranty records stay intact.
