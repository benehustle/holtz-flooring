Engineered timber installation in Melbourne

Engineered timber punishes poor installation immediately. Twenty-plus years in the trade means disciplined acclimation, moisture testing, and subfloor preparation before the first board is fixed.

The premium surface

Architects, interior designers, and high-end retailers specify engineered timber where the visual standard must hold under scrutiny. Gapping, cupping, or noisy fixings are not issues you can paint over — they trace back to preparation and environmental control.

We treat every engineered package as a technical install: documented moisture readings, staged acclimation on site, and fixings or adhesive systems matched to manufacturer guidance and Australian building conditions.

What is involved

Subfloor flatness and structural movement are assessed first. We map expansion requirements at large openings and long runs, then programme acclimation so the product stabilises before fixing begins. Adhesive spreads, secret nailing, or floating systems follow the supplier’s detail — not a generic method.

Where services or plant rooms create humidity swings, we discuss conditioning with your services contractor so the finished floor does not move outside design expectations after handover.

What to expect

Pre-start sign-off on moisture and preparation, a controlled install sequence, and protection advice for following trades. You receive a handover checklist aligned to what your superintendent or retailer client expects on premium projects.

Benefits for your project

  • Moisture discipline and acclimation managed to manufacturer and site conditions
  • Premium finishing detail suitable for design-led commercial and retail spaces
  • Reduced callback risk on high-visibility timber floors

Frequently asked questions

How long should engineered timber acclimatise on a Melbourne site?

Follow the manufacturer’s stated period and the in-service climate once the building is weathertight. We record delivery condition and on-site storage, then agree a start date that does not compress acclimation below what the warranty requires.

Do you install over in-slab heating?

Where the product is certified for embedded heating, we commission in line with supplier limits and document operating temperatures. If the product is not rated, we advise before procurement proceeds so liability stays with the correct specification.

Can retailers contract installation directly while the builder holds the site?

Yes, with a clear site authority and access window agreed with the head contractor. We coordinate inductions, protection, and handover so the retailer’s programme lines up with builder rules and safety requirements on Australian sites.
