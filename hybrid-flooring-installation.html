Hybrid flooring installation in Melbourne

Hybrid is a popular choice across many different applications, including single dwellings, multi-res and some commercial applications. We install to manufacturer tolerances for raft sizes, expansion requirements and substrate condition.

Why customers specify hybrid

Hybrid flooring can be a stable product when installation follows manufacturers’ specifications for installation. That stability matters in traditional residential application, multi-res and some commercial installations.

The product only performs when installation respects click-system limits, perimeter expansion, and flatness requirements. We work from your product data sheets, not guesswork on site.

What is involved

We confirm substrate moisture and level against manufacturer limits, plan expansion gaps at perimeters and sequence rooms so traffic does not compromise locked joints. Transitions to other finishes are set out before the first board goes down.

Where slabs or screeds are outside tolerance, we coordinate preparation early so your programme does not absorb rework during the finishing trades.

What to expect

A single site contact, a clear and concise installation timeframe, consistent communication throughout the install with photos of all works completed delivered to you after the installation is complete.

Benefits for your project

  • Technical discipline on click systems, expansion, and substrate requirements
  • Commercial-grade pacing for multi-room and multi-level programmes
  • Clear communication with retailers and site teams throughout the install window

Frequently asked questions

Do you install hybrid direct to concrete in commercial projects?

Only when the slab meets the manufacturer’s moisture and flatness requirements. We test and document as required, then recommend preparation if results sit outside specification.

Can you align installation with a retailer’s customer handover date?

Yes. Share your critical dates and we lock a sequence that preserves quality. If preparatory work slips, we flag the impact early so you can adjust the retail schedule rather than absorb silent defects.

Who supplies trims and accessories on a hybrid install?

Usually the retailer or builder’s supply chain, consistent with our installation-only model. We advise compatible profiles and heights so orders match what is on site before we mobilise labour.
