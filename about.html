Our story

At Holtz Flooring, we provide high-quality flooring installation with a focus on precision and reliability. Backed by over 20 years of experience, we deliver finishes that integrate into a space and maintain their standard over time.

We are not a retail showroom - we are dedicated flooring installation specialists. This allows us to focus entirely on expert installation across a wide range of surfaces, including hybrid, laminate, engineered timber, and vinyl plank flooring. Every project is delivered with care, attention to detail, and a commitment to getting it right the first time.

With two decades in the trade, we have worked across everything from residential projects to large-scale commercial work. We bring a practical, organised approach to every installation: ensuring the project is delivered on time, within budget, and to a standard that exceeds expectations.

Ideal clients include flooring retailers who need a dependable installer, commercial builders managing programme risk, and project managers who require clear communication and a single accountable trade.

What we don't offer

Holtz Flooring is an installation specialist. We do not sell flooring products. If you need product supply, your retailer or supplier can refer you to us for the installation component. We work alongside retailers, not in competition with them.
