Our story

At Holtz Flooring, we provide high-quality flooring installation with a focus on precision and reliability. Backed by proven experience, we deliver finishes that integrate into a space and maintain their standard over time.

We are not a retail showroom, we are dedicated flooring installation specialists. This allows us to focus entirely on expert installation across a wide range of surfaces, including hybrid, engineered timber, laminate and vinyl plank flooring. Every install is delivered with care, attention to detail and a commitment to getting it right the first time.

With two decades in the trade, we have worked across everything from residential projects to large-scale commercial work. We bring a practical, organised approach to every installation, ensuring the project is delivered on time, within budget and to a standard that exceeds expectations.
