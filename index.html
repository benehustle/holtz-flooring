About Holtz Flooring

We partner with flooring retailers and commercial teams who need installation done precisely, on time and without product conflicts. Our clients range from retail chains installing in residential homes to head contractors delivering multi-level fit-outs.

We are not a retail showroom, we are dedicated installation specialists. That focus means expert work across hybrid, engineered timber, laminate and vinyl plank, with subfloor preparation when required.

Backed by over 20 years in the trade, we bring a practical, organised approach to every installation, on time, within budget and to a standard that always meets manufacturers’ specifications.
