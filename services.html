Commercial flooring installation in Melbourne

Retailers keep margin on product. Builders keep control of programme. We deliver the installation component : organised, on time, and to a standard that holds up under inspection.

What we install

Unreliable subcontractors, variable finish quality, and poor site communication are common pain points on flooring packages. Holtz Flooring exists to remove that risk for flooring retailers, commercial builders, and project managers across Melbourne and Victoria.

We do not retail flooring. We install what your supply chain delivers: hybrid, engineered timber, laminate, vinyl plank, and we prepare substrates so those products perform as specified. That single focus keeps programmes moving and reduces callbacks.

Whether you need a retail rollout, a commercial fit-out, or a staged builder programme, we align to your dates, document progress clearly, and finish ready for handover.

What we don't offer

Holtz Flooring is an installation specialist. We do not sell flooring products. If you need product supply, your retailer or supplier can refer you to us for the installation component. We work alongside retailers, not in competition with them.
