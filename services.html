Flooring installation in Melbourne

Retailers keep margin on product. Builders keep control of the schedule. We deliver the installation component—organised, on time and to a standard that holds up under inspection.

What we install

Unreliable subcontractors, variable finish quality, and poor site communication are common pain points on flooring installations. Holtz Flooring exists to remove that risk for flooring retailers, builders and industry professionals across Melbourne and Victoria.

We do not retail flooring. We install the products that you have requested or specified—hybrid, engineered timber, laminate, vinyl plank and we prepare substrates so those products perform as the manufacturer expects. That single focus keeps programmes moving and reduces callbacks.

Whether you need a retail rollout, a commercial fit-out, or a staged builder programme, we align to your dates, document progress clearly and finish ready for handover.
